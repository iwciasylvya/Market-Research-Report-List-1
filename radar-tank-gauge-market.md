<p><ul><li>Non-contact Systems</li><li>Contact Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radar Tank Gauge market comprises two main types: non-contact systems and contact systems. Non-contact systems utilize radar waves to measure liquid levels without physical contact, ensuring accurate readings without contamination risks. These systems are ideal for harsh environments. On the other hand, contact systems involve sensors that come into direct contact with the liquid, providing detailed information but potentially introducing risks of wear or contamination. Both types serve various industries, including oil and gas, water management, and chemicals.</p></p>
