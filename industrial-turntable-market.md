<p><p>The industrial turntable market comprises two main types: powered and manual industrial turntables. Powered industrial turntables utilize electric or hydraulic systems to rotate heavy loads with ease, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor intensity in manufacturing and logistics. In contrast, manual industrial turntables rely on human effort for rotation, offering a cost-effective solution for lighter loads or less frequent use. Both types serve distinct operational needs, catering to various industries such as warehousing, construction, and food processing.</p></p>
