<p><p>The Xenon Lamp Spectrophotometer market encompasses various types of instruments used for analyzing materials. UV-visible Spectrophotometers assess light absorption in the ultraviolet and visible range, while Infrared Spectrophotometers evaluate molecular vibrations through infrared light interaction. Lastly, Visible Spectrophotometers specifically analyze light in the visible spectrum. Each type serves distinct applications across scientific and industrial fields.</p></p>
