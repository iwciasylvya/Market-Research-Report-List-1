<p><p>The exfoliating masks market comprises two main types: patch masks and smear masks. Patch masks are pre-cut, adhesive applications that target specific areas, offering convenience and precision for exfoliation, often infused with active ingredients for enhanced benefits. Smear masks, on the other hand, are applied directly to the skin and need to be spread evenly; they cover larger areas and allow for customized exfoliation experiences. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences in skincare routines, promoting healthier, smoother skin.</p></p>
