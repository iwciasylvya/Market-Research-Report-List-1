<p><strong>The Volume Bragg Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Volume Bragg Grating (VBG) market includes various types: </p><p>1. Reflective Bragg Gratings (RBG) reflect specific wavelengths, ideal for lasers and sensing applications.</p><p>2. Transmission Bragg Gratings (TBG) transmit light while reflecting certain wavelengths, used in optical filtering.</p><p>3. Cavity Bragg Gratings (CBG) enhance cavity performance in lasers, optimizing output.</p><p>4. Bandwidth-Narrowing Filters (BNF) narrow spectral bandwidth for improved resolution in optical systems. </p><p>These types cater to diverse applications in telecommunications, lasers, and sensing technologies.</p></p>
