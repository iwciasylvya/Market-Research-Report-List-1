<p><p>Flange couplings are mechanical devices used to connect two shafts, ensuring efficient transmission of torque. Rigid flange couplings provide a strong, inflexible connection that is ideal for precise alignment and high torque applications, but they lack the ability to accommodate misalignment. In contrast, flexible flange couplings allow for some degree of misalignment and vibration absorption, making them suitable for dynamic applications. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring optimal performance in rotating machinery and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
