<p><p>The conventional contact lenses market comprises three main types: soft lenses, hard lenses, and rigid gas permeable (RGP) lenses. Soft lenses are popular for their comfort and flexibility, suitable for daily wear. Hard lenses, though less common now, offer durability and excellent vision correction. RGP lenses combine the benefits of both, providing clear vision and oxygen permeability while maintaining a rigid shape. Each type serves different needs, influencing user preference and market dynamics.</p></p>
