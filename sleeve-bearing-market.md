<p><ul><li>Radial Sleeve Bearing</li><li>Axial Sleeve Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sleeve bearing market comprises two main types: radial and axial sleeve bearings. Radial sleeve bearings support radial loads and are commonly used in applications where the shaft rotates perpendicular to the load direction, providing smooth motion and reducing friction. Axial sleeve bearings, on the other hand, support axial loads and are designed for applications where the load is parallel to the shaft, often employed in thrust applications. Both types are essential in various industries for efficient machinery operation.</p></p>
