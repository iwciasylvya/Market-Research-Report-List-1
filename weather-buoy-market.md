<p><p>Weather buoy markets are categorized into solar-powered and battery-powered types, each serving distinct operational needs. Solar-powered buoys utilize solar panels for energy, making them environmentally friendly and ideal for remote locations with adequate sunlight. They offer continuous operation without the need for battery replacement. In contrast, battery-powered buoys rely on stored energy, making them versatile for areas with limited sunlight but requiring regular maintenance for battery replacement. Both types play crucial roles in marine data collection and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
