<p><ul><li>Vacuum Booster</li><li>Hydraulic Booster</li><li>Air Pressure Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake booster market encompasses three main types: vacuum boosters, hydraulic boosters, and air pressure boosters. Vacuum boosters use engine vacuum to enhance braking force, offering efficiency in traditional vehicles. Hydraulic boosters employ hydraulic fluid pressure to amplify braking power, often found in high-performance and heavy-duty applications. Air pressure boosters utilize compressed air for braking assistance, primarily in heavy vehicles like trucks and buses. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements, enhancing safety and performance in braking systems.</p></p>
