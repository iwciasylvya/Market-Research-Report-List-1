<p><p>Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) is primarily categorized into two types: kerosene-type fuels and naphtha-type fuels. Kerosene-type fuels, including Jet A and Jet A-1, are widely used for commercial aviation due to their high energy density and low freezing point, making them suitable for high-altitude flights. Naphtha-type fuels, on the other hand, are less common but can be used in specialized or older aircraft engines. Both fuel types are essential for ensuring optimal performance and safety in aviation operations.</p></p>
