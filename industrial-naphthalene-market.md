<p><p>Industrial naphthalene is a white crystalline hydrocarbon derived from coal tar or petroleum. The Asia-Pacific region, particularly China and India, is a key driver of this growth due to rapid industrialization and urbanization.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include a shift towards sustainable production methods and the development of bio-based naphthalene alternatives.
