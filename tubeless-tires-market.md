<p><p>The tubeless tire market comprises two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, offering improved grip, fuel efficiency, and a smooth ride, making them popular for passenger vehicles. Bias tires, on the other hand, have layers that crisscross, providing sturdiness and flexibility, often preferred for off-road or heavy-duty applications. The choice between these tire types largely depends on the specific performance needs and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
