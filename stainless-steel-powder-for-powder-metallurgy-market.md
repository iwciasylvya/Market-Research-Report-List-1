<p><p>The stainless steel powder for powder metallurgy market comprises three main types: austenitic, martensitic, and ferritic grades. Austenitic grades are known for their excellent corrosion resistance and high ductility, making them suitable for various applications. Martensitic grades offer high strength and hardness, often utilized in tools and structural components. Ferritic grades are characterized by good resistance to oxidation and stress corrosion cracking, favored in automotive and industrial applications. Each grade serves distinct needs in the powder metallurgy landscape.</p></p>
