<p><p>The PCR workstation market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Multiplex PCR enables the simultaneous amplification of multiple targets, enhancing efficiency. Single-cell PCR focuses on analyzing individual cells, crucial for single-cell genomics. Long-range PCR allows amplifying larger DNA segments, useful in structural analysis. Methylation-specific PCR (MSP) targets methylated DNA, vital for epigenetic studies. Fast-cycling PCR significantly reduces amplification times, improving throughput. Additionally, other specialized PCR techniques address diverse research needs, advancing molecular biology and genetic testing.</p></p>
