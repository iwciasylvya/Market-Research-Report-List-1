<p><p>The piezoceramic components for the automotive market are primarily categorized into two types: single plate piezo ceramics and multilayer piezo ceramics. Single plate piezo ceramics are known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in basic sensors and actuators. In contrast, multilayer piezo ceramics offer enhanced performance due to their ability to generate greater displacement and force in a compact design, making them ideal for advanced applications like fuel injection systems and active suspension controls in modern vehicles.</p></p>
