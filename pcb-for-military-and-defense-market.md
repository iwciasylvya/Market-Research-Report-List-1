<p><p>The military and defense market employs three main types of PCBs: rigid, flexible, and rigid-flex. Rigid PCBs are inflexible and provide robust support for high-density components, ideal for large systems. Flexible PCBs allow for complex designs and better space optimization, enabling lightweight, compact devices. Rigid-flex PCBs combine the advantages of both, offering durability and flexibility, suitable for applications requiring both stability and dynamic configurations. These types enhance performance, reliability, and adaptability in demanding military environments.</p></p>
