<p><strong>The Domestic Smoke Alarms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ionisation Type</li><li>Photoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The domestic smoke alarms market comprises two main types: ionisation and photoelectric. Ionisation smoke alarms are highly sensitive to fast-burning fires and utilize radioactive material to detect smoke. In contrast, photoelectric alarms are more effective for smoldering fires, using a light beam that is disrupted by smoke particles. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing home safety, catering to different fire detection needs, and are often used in combination to provide comprehensive protection against various fire scenarios.</p></p>
