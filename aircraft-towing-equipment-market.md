<p><strong>The Aircraft Towing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Towbar Tractors</li><li>Towbarless Tractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft towing equipment market comprises two main types: towbar tractors and towbarless tractors. Towbar tractors utilize a rigid towbar attachment to connect with the aircraft, providing a cost-effective solution for small to medium-sized aircraft. They require precise maneuvering for attachment. In contrast, towbarless tractors use a platform design that allows for direct coupling to the aircraft, offering greater flexibility and ease of use, particularly for larger aircraft, and facilitating smoother operations in busy airport environments.</p></p>
